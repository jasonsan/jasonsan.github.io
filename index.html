About HTS

At HTS Accounting Services, a trusted CPA firm, we are dedicated to supporting small and medium-sized businesses, as well as individuals, with expert bookkeeping, payroll, and tax return services. Our mission is to provide you with peace of mind through professional, reliable financial solutions, allowing you to focus on what matters most - growing your business and enjoying life.

We pride ourselves on offering personalized solutions tailored to meet your unique financial needs. Whether you're in the construction, manufacturing, or service industry, our team is here to help you stay financially confident and prepared for any audits or challenges that may arise.

Partner with HTS Accounting Services and take the next step toward achieving your financial success with confidence and ease.

Our Services

1. Bookkeeping and Payroll Services

  • Accounts Receivable: Efficiently managing customer invoices and collections.
  • Accounts Payable: Ensuring timely and accurate payments to vendors.
  • Bank Reconciliation: Keeping your financial records accurate and up to date.
  • Payroll and Statutory Remittance: Handling payroll processing and compliance with regulatory requirements.

We offer comprehensive bookkeeping and payroll services tailored to your needs, whether you use QuickBooks, Sage, Xero, or other accounting software. If you're not ready to invest in software, we can deliver these services using Excel, ensuring flexibility and convenience.

Let the experts handle your financial processes so you can focus on what truly matters—growing your business and enjoying life.

2. Accounting Services

  • Financial Reporting: Gain clear and accurate insights into your business's financial health with timely, reliable reports.
  • Costing Methods: Make informed business decisions with customized costing reports and methodologies.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Navigate complex accounting regulations with ease and confidence.
  • Strategic Decision-Making Support: Empower your business strategies with data-driven insights.

At HTS, we deliver accurate and timely financial reports that provide valuable insights for your business. Our solutions help reduce costs and ensure your financial decisions are backed by robust data.
