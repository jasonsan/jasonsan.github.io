About HTS

At HTS Accounting Services, we are dedicated to supporting small and medium-sized businesses with expert bookkeeping, payroll, and tax return services.

Our mission is to provide you with peace of mind through professional, reliable financial solutions, allowing you to focus on what matters most - growing your business and enjoying life.

We pride ourselves on offering personalized solutions tailored to meet your unique financial needs. Whether you're in the construction, manufacturing, or service industry, our team is here to help you stay financially confident and prepared for any audits or challenges that may arise.
